About Morse Code...
Morse Code is the conversion of the latin alphabet into a series of dots and dashes (.-). The length of their sequence is based on their frequency of use. As E is the most commonly used letter it is the shortest sequence being just a single dash ("-"). The simplisity, usability and accessability of this code makes it the most versitile form of encriptionneeding only a light source, radio or even a table to tap. the fact that it is so well known makes it useless to secure information but is extremely useful to convey messages such as an SOS code.
